
**Toy Store KPI Dashboard – Tracking Key Business Metrics and Exploring Sales Performance Trends**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This interactive **Toy Store KPI Dashboard** was developed to monitor key business performance indicators and provide actionable insights into sales, revenue, profit, product performance, and store location trends. The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly evaluate overall business health, identify high-performing categories, and analyze revenue patterns over time.

The report focuses on helping management make data-driven decisions by presenting critical KPIs in a visually appealing and easy-to-understand format.

---

## 🎯 Purpose of the Dashboard

The primary objectives of this dashboard are:

* Monitor overall business performance through key metrics.
* Track sales, revenue, and profit trends over time.
* Compare product category performance.
* Analyze store location-specific performance.
* Identify profitable product segments and business opportunities.
* Support strategic decision-making with real-time insights.

## 📈 Key Insights Derived

### Overall Business Performance

* The store generated **24,927 orders**.
* Total revenue reached **$388K+**.
* Total profit exceeded **$105K**, indicating healthy profitability.

### Product Category Analysis

* **Art & Crafts** is the top-performing category in terms of order volume.
* **Toys** closely follows and contributes significantly to sales.
* **Electronics** records the lowest order volume among all categories.

### Revenue Trend Analysis

* Revenue remains relatively stable with periodic spikes.
* Certain periods show increased customer purchasing activity.
* Revenue fluctuations suggest opportunities for promotional campaigns during lower-performing periods.

### Location-Based Analysis

* The selected **Downtown** store location contributes to the displayed metrics.
* Management can compare locations using the slicer to identify high-performing branches.
